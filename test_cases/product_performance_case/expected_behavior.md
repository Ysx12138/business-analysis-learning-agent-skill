# Product Performance Case - Expected Behavior

The Agent should:

- Inspect key product, category, and time fields.
- Define questions about ranking, profitability, and category comparison.
- Analyze product ranking by revenue and profit.
- Compare sales volume vs margin.
- Explain why high sales may not imply high profit.
- Provide business interpretation for major findings.
- Explain relevant metrics and abbreviations clearly.
- Distinguish data evidence from assumptions.
- Avoid unsupported causal claims.
