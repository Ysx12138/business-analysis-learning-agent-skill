# Metric Glossary Template

| Metric | Full Name | Meaning | Formula | Business Use | Common Misunderstanding |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| KPI | Key Performance Indicator | A metric used to evaluate whether a business goal is being achieved | Depends on the goal | Tracks performance | KPI is not one fixed metric |
| GMV | Gross Merchandise Volume | Total transaction value before deductions | Sum of transaction value | Measures transaction scale | GMV is not equal to revenue or profit |
| AOV | Average Order Value | Average spending per order | Revenue / Number of Orders | Measures customer spending level | Higher AOV is not always better if margin is low |
| ROI | Return on Investment | Return compared with cost | Profit / Cost | Evaluates investment efficiency | ROI may ignore time and risk |
| CAC | Customer Acquisition Cost | Cost to acquire one customer | Marketing Cost / New Customers | Evaluates acquisition efficiency | Lower CAC is not always better if customer quality is poor |
| Retention Rate | Retention Rate | Percentage of users who continue using or buying | Retained Users / Total Users | Measures customer stickiness | High retention still needs revenue quality |
| Conversion Rate | Conversion Rate | Percentage of users who complete a target action | Conversions / Visitors | Measures funnel efficiency | Conversion rate alone does not show profit |
| Contribution Rate | Contribution Rate | Share of a category or item in the total | Part / Total | Identifies major contributors | High contribution may also mean high dependency risk |
| Profit Margin | Profit Margin | Percentage of profit in revenue | Profit / Revenue | Measures profitability | High revenue does not mean high margin |
