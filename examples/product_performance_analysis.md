# Product Performance Analysis Example

## User Request

"Analyze product performance and explain what the results mean for decisions."

## Dataset Description

Example fields:

- product_id
- product_name
- category
- sales_amount
- quantity
- cost
- profit
- order_date

## Expected Agent Behavior

Agent should:

- inspect product and category fields
- compare product ranking by revenue and profit
- explain volume vs margin trade-offs
- interpret category differences
- provide beginner-friendly explanations

## Sample Output Outline

- Task understanding and overview
- Business questions and methods
- Product and category findings
- Revenue vs profit interpretation
- Beginner learning notes

## Why This Example Matters

It shows that high sales does not always mean high profit, which is a core beginner lesson.
